Online Accommodation are not always what thay seem

Every day, people show up at hotels and short-term rentals only to discover that they are nothing like their online profile. Their ocean view looks out at a parking lot. The internet is spotty or nonexistent. Their accommodations feel cramped and not as clean as they should be. The travel experts at TZORT want to prevent these headaches.

Resort-style travel is simply a different story. When you arrive at your suite, you will find it perfectly prepared for your stay. You will have all the comforts of a house with a full kitchen and washer and dryer. At the same time, you will enjoy resort amenities like an onsite experience manager, 24-hour security, and relaxing pools and spas.

At TZORT, our online travel agency only works with exclusive resorts that meet the highest standards of cleanliness and hospitality. We take the stress out of online booking by providing accurate descriptions and a responsive team of travel agents. We think everyone should have the opportunity to travel resort style.
